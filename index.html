Paydiant Offer Portal Guide
hiddenanchor {.anchor #intro} ## Introduction to the Paydiant Campaign Portal ## The Paydiant Campaign Portal is a web-based application that enables Paydiant Merchants to design and publish offers and campaigns. The mobile wallet Issuers can use offers to incentivize their mobile wallet users to increase mobile payment transactions. test The following illustration shows the basic workflow for Offers. ![Introduction](./images/architecture_portal.png) {.single} Depending on the design of the offer campaign, a mobile user can be offered a special incentive to buy something at a particular Merchant or to conduct business at a particular time of day or be encouraged to use the phone in order to get a discount or other benefit. The Paydiant Campaign Portal provides an Offer management environment that can be used to gather the pieces needed to construct the offer to be displayed in a mobile wallet, to defines the parameters of how the offer campaign is conducted, and to publish the offers to multiple merchants simultaneously. This Help System describes: - How to access the Campaign Portal - How user roles define security for Offers - Creating and editing user roles - Creating and editing offer campaigns - How to specify locations for an offer - How to target specific customers
